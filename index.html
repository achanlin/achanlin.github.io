A. L. Rouxell

Author of Tree of Life, the first book of a fantasy series blending mystery and adventure: Legend of the East.

A.L. Rouxell lives in a tree house nestled within a mountain. From this unconventional abode, they connect with the world through the wonders of the internet, regularly descending to gather supplies and reconnect with the people they love.

Oh, how Rouxell wishes this were true!

An essential part of Rouxell's life is reading and writing, with a diverse taste spanning classics, mystery, fantasy, science fiction, and more. Besides reading and writing, Rouxell loves math, building Lego structures, solving Rubik's cubes, tackling sudoku puzzles, and playing video games.
