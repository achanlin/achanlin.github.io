A.L. Rouxell is the pen name of a writing duo—a parent and their young co-creator—each bringing their own unique strengths, imaginations, and perspectives. United by a love of reading, writing, puzzle-solving, and a deep curiosity about the world, they first began this book as a fun activity, simply creating something they both loved. Over time, it grew into a project and, eventually, a complete book. This is their first collaboration, with more to come.
