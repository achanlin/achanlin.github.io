Four teenagers - Tyson, Lucas, Mia, and Momo - find themselves stranded in the middle of the ocean. How did they end up there, and what is the reason behind it? Do they have their own hidden agenda? Could there be some mysterious force at play?

Tyson and Lucas's unbreakable friendship is put to the ultimate test in a life-or-death situation. Mia, the brilliant overachiever, finds herself confronted with challenges that will profoundly alter her life. And Momo, the mysterious, gentle, and kind-hearted soul...

A strange animal lurks in the shadows, driven by a singular cry: Revenge! Who or what is it?

Somewhere on the vast ocean, the most luxurious and expensive expedition cruise superyacht in the world glides gracefully through the water. Its owner, Donal Bull, proclaims, "To explore the furthest corners of the world, to boldly go where no one has gone before, and to do so in utmost comfort." But what dark secret is Donal Bull hiding? What fate awaits him?

Join Tyson, Lucas, Mia, and Momo on a thrilling journey of survival, friendship, and discovery.
